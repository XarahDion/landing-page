Pretty places I had the chance to visit

Here are a few pictures I took during my tour in Europe in 2017. The image to the right in a gorge nearing the Alps in Grenoble, France.

Four contrasting places.

Prague is the capital city of Czech. It has Vltava river running across and a vibrant cultural life.

Bamberg is a quaint medieval Unesco town in Bavaria, Germany. You can find there outstanding architectural sights.

The Limat river in the heart of Zurich's metropolitan area is a favorite summer destination for locals.

Wroclaw, southern Poland, is also a Unesco heritage site with it's main market square and stunning decor.
