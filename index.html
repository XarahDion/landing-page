Pretty places I had the chance to visit

Here are a few pictures I took during my tour in Europe in 2017. The image to the right is of a gorge nearing the Alps in Grenoble, France.

Four contrasting places.

Vibrant with history and cultural life, Prague is the capital city of Czech. The Vltava river runs across its heart.

Bamberg is a quaint medieval Unesco town in Bavaria, Germany, with outstanding architectural sights.

The charming Limat river in the heart of Zurich's metropolitan area is a favorite summer destination for locals.

Wroclaw, southern Poland, is also a Unesco heritage site with it's main market square and stunning decor.

” The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes, but in having new eyes.”

- Marcel Proust
